## What this page does

Barcode is a lightweight scanner for traditional 1D/2D barcodes using the handheld reader's built-in barcode scanner (or the phone camera on mobile). A mode dropdown picks between **Single** and continuous capture; **Start** arms the scanner, **Clear** empties the list, and **Export CSV** downloads everything scanned so far. The empty state reads "No barcodes scanned. Press and hold the device trigger button to scan."

## How it fits in the app

Barcode covers the gap where an asset has a printed SKU or serial but no RFID tag yet — useful during onboarding, receiving, or spot-checks against paper labels. The CSV export is the handoff point to **Assets** or to external systems.
