## What this page does

Locate is a proximity finder for a single tag. You paste a **Tag EPC Identifier** into the field, press **Start**, and then walk around while the page updates a semicircular **Signal Strength** gauge in real time — stronger signal means the tag is closer. The **Statistics** panel tracks Current / Average (1s) / Peak readings, update rate in Hz, and an overall Status (Idle / Scanning). An **Audio Feedback** toggle plays a rising tone as you get warmer, so you don't need to look at the screen while hunting.

## How it fits in the app

Locate complements Inventory: Inventory tells you what's in the room; Locate narrows in on a single item that Inventory flagged as present but hasn't been visually confirmed. Tags are usually copied here from the Inventory results list or from an **Assets** record.
