## What this page does

Assets is the catalog of things you're tracking. A search field, a locations filter, and a sort-by dropdown sit above the list, with a floating **+** button and a **Create Asset** call-to-action in the middle of the empty state ("No assets yet — Get started by adding your first asset"). Footer cards summarize counts: **Total Assets**, **Active** (in use), and **Inactive** (not in use). A **Share** button in the top-right allows handing the filtered list off to teammates.

## How it fits in the app

Assets is the stable record that scanning workflows refer back to: each asset has at least one RFID tag EPC and optionally a barcode, a home location, and metadata. **Inventory** and **Locate** match scanned tags against these records to produce "Found / Missing" counts; **Reports** rolls movement history up per asset.
