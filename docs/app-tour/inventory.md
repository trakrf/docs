## What this page does

Inventory is the workhorse scanning screen. You press and hold the handheld reader's trigger and every RFID tag in range is added to the **Scanned** list in real time. The top toolbar holds the core scanning controls (**Start**, **Sample**, **Reconcile**, **Clear**, audio toggle, **Share**, **Save**), and the footer cards summarize the live scan — **Found**, **Missing**, **Not Listed**, **Total Scanned**, and **Saveable** (recognized assets). Without a paired reader the screen shows a "No items scanned" empty state, a yellow "Supported browsers: Chrome, Edge, Opera" banner, and a **Connect Device** button.

## How it fits in the app

Inventory answers "what's in front of me right now?" by comparing the live RFID sweep against a CSV or saved asset list; the counts in the footer make mismatches obvious at a glance. From Inventory you typically move to **Locate** to narrow in on a specific item, or to **Assets** to turn newly-scanned tags into managed records.
